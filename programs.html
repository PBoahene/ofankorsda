Our Programs & Activities

Discover a vibrant calendar of spiritual, educational, and community-focused programs designed to nurture faith, foster fellowship, and serve our community. There’s something for everyone at Ofankor SDA Church!

Bible Study

Deepen your understanding of God's word every Sunday evening with interactive group studies and discussions.

Youth Fellowship

Empowering the next generation through mentorship, music, outreach, and fun activities every Sabbath afternoon.

Community Service

Join us as we reach out to our neighbors with acts of kindness, health screenings, and support for those in need.

Prayer Meetings

Experience the power of prayer every Wednesday evening as we intercede for our church, families, and world.

Music Ministry

Be uplifted by our choirs and praise teams, or join in to share your musical gifts for God’s glory.

Children’s Ministry

Fun, faith-filled activities and Bible lessons for children of all ages every Sabbath morning.

Family Life

Workshops, seminars, and events to strengthen marriages, parenting, and family relationships.

Pathfinders & Adventurers

Adventure, leadership, and spiritual growth for children and teens through camping, honors, and service.

About Us

Ofankor SDA Church is committed to spreading the gospel and serving our community through Christ-centered ministry.
