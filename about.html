About Our Church

We are a vibrant fellowship of Seventh-day Adventist believers, dedicated to spiritual growth, effective witnessing, and academic excellence.

A Community of Faith, Fellowship, and Service

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ who live as His loving witnesses and proclaim to all people the everlasting gospel of the Three Angels' Messages in preparation for His soon return.

Our Vision

To be a thriving Adventist community where people experience Christ's transforming power and share His love with others.

Our Rich Heritage

Our Foundation

Founded in 1863 in the United States, the Seventh-day Adventist Church emerged from the Millerite movement, establishing a foundation of faith and purpose that continues to guide us today.

Prophetic Guidance

Ellen G. White, a key figure in our church's history, provided spiritual guidance through her writings, shaping our doctrine and mission with inspired wisdom and counsel.

Global Impact

From our humble beginnings, we've experienced rapid global expansion through education, healthcare, and missionary work, touching lives across cultures and continents.
